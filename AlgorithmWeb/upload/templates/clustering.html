    Classification of scRNA-seq data clustering methods :

    Distance-based methods use distance-based clustering algorithms such as k-means and hierarchical clustering. Density-based methods employ the density-based clustering mechanism such as the DBSCAN algorithm and its variants. Graph-based methods first transform the data to a graph, over which a graph clustering algorithm is applied. The probability model-based methods cluster data based on a certain probability distribution or process. The matrix-based methods first transform the data to a matrix, and perform matrix split-ting or decomposition to cluster the data. The Deep learning-based methods learn the latent structure of features based on Denoising Auto-encoders or Variational Autoencoders, then clustering data on the latent space.
